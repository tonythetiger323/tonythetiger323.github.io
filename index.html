About Me

Tony Greeley was born March 23, 1982 at Central DuPage Hospital in Winfield, IL. By 1st grade his family moved to Wheaton, IL where he grew up, graduating from Wheaton Warrenville South High School in June of 2000.

Tony was last a Genius (aka Apple Certified Mac Technician) at Apple Lincoln Park and has worked there for 8 years as of July 12th, 2018. Tony spent 1.5 of those years working in sales and the remaing time has been in tech support.

Tony is recent graduate of Northwestern University's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp and has always been a more of a logic and analytics person then he has a visual and creative one, so naturally, he has taken to prefering back-end development over front-end. Of the back-end side of things, Tony's main interests lie with establishing the server and app routing rather than database management as he prefers using logic to problem solve over just processing data.

In additon to being a bit of a tech nerd, Tony is also a big Chicago Cubs and Blackhawks fan, loves all things of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (proud member of Team Cap), amature photographer with a love for city scapes, and lover of house music. Tony is also a proud Uncle and has 2 nephews, Luke and Noah, and a niece, Fatih.
